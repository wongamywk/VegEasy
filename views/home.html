About Veg Easy

Tentatively opening in the end of 2017, Veg Easy, a new vegetarian restaurant will be launched in Wan Chai. We will do our best to bring everyone of you another extraordinary experience in meatless dishes, together with our top quality of services. We will cater to our customers with a menu of International vegetarian cuisines which includes soups, salads, main dishes and our special coffee and floral tea. Our menu is going to change frequently to reflect the best produce of the season.

Our Core Values


{Healthy} - We serve meatless meals using 100% natural and healthy ingredients and free from animals.

{Service} - Every customer will be served as our VIP, a friend and a partner.

{Happiness} - Provide joyful experiences for customers, employees, friends, and families.
